Mission

FASTER creates, connects, and cultivates a valuable movement of Filipino Americans in the Silicon Valley tech community, located in the San Francisco Bay Area.

We attract, advance, and excel the network of Filipino Americans in Silicon Valley technology companies in a range of professions, including but not limited to: entrepreneurs, investors, engineers, designers.

1

Education

Through our mentorship programs with local universities, we strive to build a pipeline of new talent into tech companies in the SF Bay Area. Apply to be a mentor here.

2

Professional

We strive to create an open space for collaboration among like-minded groups including Filipino and Asian American employee resource groups at tech companies, community meetups, and other Filipino American nonprofit organizations in the tech industry.

3

Social Media

We are building our network through various social media channels to help centralize our enriching community.

Our Story

Co-founded by Erin Jerri Malonzo Pangilinan and Carlos Miguel Lasa, the concept of FASTER was to continue the growth of the local Filipino American community in the SF Bay Area in tech industry.

FASTERCON

FASTERCON is the inaugural annual conference hosted at UC Berkeley by FASTER (Filipino Americans in Silicon Valley Tech) which aims to create, connect, and cultivate a valuable movement of Filipino Americans in the technology industry, with a focus on the San Francisco Bay Area. This event is co-organized with UCB PASAE (Pilipino Association of Scientists, Architects and Engineers), STAC-SV (Science & Technology Advisory Council) and UCB PAAC (Cal Pilipino American Alumni Chapter).

This year's conference is focused on highlighting and strengthening the network of Filipino American technology professionals in the Bay Area. The agenda will include several keynote speeches on the future of Filipino Americans in tech, workshops on entrepreneurship and design, and panel discussions on engineering and professional development for underrepresented communities in the industry.

This event is open to all - students, technology professionals, entrepreneurs and allies and supporters of the Filipino American community in the Bay Area. Please follow this event page for updates -- ticket pricing, registration details and full agenda to be released soon!

What we do

-Inspire leadership: Highlight the accomplishments prominent professionals (including but not limited to, members of Filipino Employee Resource Groups at Silicon Valley tech companies) to encourage more Filipino Americans entering tech, providing a venue as a way they can give back to community.

-Leverage existing F/Pilipin@ University alumni groups to create a strategic and sustainable mentorship pipeline for existing industry professionals and those soon to become industry professionals.

-Build a sustainable pipeline of students graduating in STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math) + business/entrepreneurship interested in navigating and entering the workforce that is the tech ecosystem of Silicon Valley, give exposure to growing tech professions by self-identified and ally Filipino American industry professionals.

Inspire

We seek to increase visibility and celebrate the contributions of Filipino Americans in tech, those who inspire leadership across professions in the industry.

Organize

We seek to centralize our community by gathering, organizing, and making information on Filipino Americans in tech more accessible with an open-source community database of self-identified Filipino Americans in various professions in tech.

Give

Silicon Valley's rich history of Asian American contributions is significant, FASTER helps bring together our community of stories of Filipino American contributions to the tech industry.
