FASTER

FASTER creates, connects and cultivates a valuable movement of Filipino Americans in the Silicon Valley technology industry and the surrounding community in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Who we are

FASTER hopes to attract, advance and strengthen the network of Filipino Americans that work across Silicon Valley technology companies in a wide range of professions, including but not limited to: engineers, designers, entrepreneurs and investors.

What we do

Our growing network consists of a vibrant community of Filipino American employee resource groups at technology companies, young and seasoned tech professionals and startup founders and entrepreneurs.

We also have outreach and mentorship progams with UC Berkeley and Stanford University to encourage Filipino American youth to pursue careers in the technology industry.
