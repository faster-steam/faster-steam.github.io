About

FASTER creates, connects, and cultivates a valuable movement of Filipino Americans in the Silicon Valley tech community, located in the San Francisco Bay Area.

1

Education

Through our mentorship programs with local universities, we strive to build a pipeline of new talent into tech companies in the SF Bay Area.

2

Professional

We strive to create an open space for collaboration among like-minded groups including Filipino and Asian American employee resource groups at tech companies, community meetups, and other Filipino American nonprofit organizations in the tech industry.

3

Social Media

We are building our network through various social media channels to help centralize our enriching community.

MISSION

We attract, advance, and excel the network of Filipino Americans in Silicon Valley technology companies in a range of professions, including but not limited to: entrepreneurs, investors, engineers, designers.

Our Story

Co-founded by Erin Jerri Malonzo Pangilinan and Carlos Miguel Lasa, the concept of FASTER was to continue the growth of the local Filipino American community in the SF Bay Area in tech industry.
